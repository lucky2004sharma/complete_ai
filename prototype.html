> JPG TO PNG CONVERTER LOSSLESS_OUTPUT

USAGE & PURPOSE:

Best for converting standard photos into PNG format when you need high quality, sharp text readability, or intend to add transparency later.

HOW IT WORKS:

Decompresses standard JPG compression artifacts and encodes pixels into PNG's lossless bitmap structure.

> JPEG TO PNG CONVERTER EXT_STANDARD

USAGE & PURPOSE:

JPEG is the exact same format as JPG (Joint Photographic Experts Group). Use this for digital camera files ending in .jpeg to prevent quality degradation.

HOW IT WORKS:

Reads RGB color channels from the .jpeg container and maps them cleanly to an uncompressed Portable Network Graphics (PNG) algorithm.

> PNG TO JPEG CONVERTER MAX_COMPRESSION

USAGE & PURPOSE:

Essential for drastically reducing file size! Heavy PNG images (like screenshots or banners) become up to 80% lighter when converted to JPEG for fast website loading.

HOW IT WORKS:

Flattens transparent alpha channels onto a solid white canvas and applies DCT compression to minimize file payload.

> WEBP TO JPEG CONVERTER COMPATIBILITY

USAGE & PURPOSE:

Many government portals, old software, and document editors do not support Google's WebP format. Convert WebP to JPEG for 100% universal software compatibility.

HOW IT WORKS:

Decodes VP8/VP8L WebP bitstreams into raw canvas data and re-encodes them into standardized ISO/IEC 10918-1 JPEG format.
